About Partnership & Support

We seek financial support from you / your esteemed organization to help realise this noble initiative. Your valuable CSR contribution will directly support:

  • Procurement of medical equipment
  • Operational cost for cataract surgeries
  • Ophthalmic OPD strengthening
  • Ambulance & outreach vehicles
  • Diagnostic & surgical instruments

The project will be sustained through public contributions, government aid and partnerships with agencies.
