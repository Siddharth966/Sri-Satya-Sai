SPM Trust — community outreach & lifesaving missions

1) Sai Dhanwantri Hospital — Covid Response

Rapid, large-scale intervention during the pandemic

In a challenging situation of Covid-19, SPM Trust took initiative to meet the crisis head-on. A building of 30,000 sq.ft. was dedicated to start a full-fledged Sai Dhanwantri Covid Hospital in Yavatmal. The trust procured an oxygen concentrator plant on loan of Rs. 80 L and made available 150 beds with oxygen out of a 250-bed facility — saving hundreds of lives. Services were offered at minimum fees as per Govt. guidelines.

More details

The trust also coordinated staff, logistics and affordable treatment to ensure equitable access across densely affected communities.

2) Sri Satya Sai Narayan Seva — Feed the Hungry (Since 1995)

Sustained free meal service for caretakers & needy

Providing free meals to needy patients' relatives/caretakers at Government Medical College Yavatmal. Begun on 1st November 1995, the program runs continuously and has served more than 700,000 people. The service addresses caretakers who lose income while supporting hospitalized family members. The initiative drew media attention during its silver jubilee and remains an emblem of the trust's social commitment.

3) Eye Check-up & Surgery Camps

Thousands of cataract patients served in rural areas

Rural Eye Camps

Health check-up camps organized across the district, providing free spectacles and selecting patients for surgery.

Surgery Assistance

Free cataract and other surgical treatments offered at Yavatmal & Nagpur hospitals; over 3000 patients benefited.

Long-term Impact

By focusing on senior citizens and the underserved, the trust helped reduce avoidable blindness and restored independence to many.

4) Mother & Child Care

Free maternal care & child health services

The Trust established Satya Sai Sanjeevani Mother & Child Hospital in Yavatmal to secure healthy motherhood and childhood. Regular services provided free of cost include maternal care, antenatal care, ultrasonography, lab investigations, fetal echo-cardiography, normal & C-section deliveries, medicines, nutritional supplements, counselling and more.

Program highlights
  • Monthly regular medical camps in 3 public health centers and 1 rural hospital
  • Gynecologist examinations, free medication & protein supplements (Sai Sure)
  • Over 5000 women examined and supported since 2017

Services (selected)

Maternal care · Antenatal care · Ultrasonography · Lab tests · Obstetrics · Fetal echo · Deliveries · Medicines · Counselling

5) Heart Screening & Diagnostic Camps

Large-scale pediatric screenings and referrals

Regular heart screening camps were organized for needy children. Counseling and travel arrangements were provided; over 100,000 children were screened and 90 critical cases were diagnosed and referred for free surgery.

6) Charitable Dispensary (Since 1958)

Homeopathic services across decades

Legacy Care

A charitable homeopathic dispensary functioning for 65+ years, serving over 350,000 patients with long-term care for chronic conditions.

Common Treatments

Asthma, eczema, gastric disorders, rheumatic arthritis, chronic skin conditions and more are treated affordably and respectfully.

Community Trust

The dispensary forms an essential, trust-based healthcare pillar in the region.

7) Shri Satya Sai All Religions Mass Marriage (Since 1994)

Removing financial burden — celebrating unity

To ease the heavy financial burden of marriage on poor families the Trust conducts multi-religion mass marriages. The program covers registration, clothing, mangalsutra, utensils and meals. Over 2000 marriages supported, and the concept spread across Maharashtra with government incentives added later.

8) Sports: Building Youth & Champions

State, national & international achievements

Grassroots Clubs

Sports clubs and district associations created to encourage youth participation; over 600 players competed at state level events.

National Recognition

150 players selected to higher levels; 11 players received state awards with prize money and mementos for excellence.

International Exposure

Players participated in international street football tournaments in Germany, Australia and more — bringing pride to the region.

9) Education & Schools

Three schools from primary to board exams — inclusive & holistic

The Trust runs three schools emphasizing cultural grooming, academic excellence and all-round development. Admissions are inclusive regardless of caste, creed or economic status. "When one educates many, one can change the world."

10) Disaster Relief & Environment

Relief across national disasters and local green drives

National Calamity Response

Teams were sent to Gujarat (Bhuj earthquake), Orissa cyclone, Nagapattam tsunami, and more; recognized by local officials for prompt action.

Local Flood Relief

During Kolhapur, Sangli and Yavatmal floods, students and volunteers collected and delivered essentials to affected families.

Tree Planting & Waste Programs

One tree per student drives, plastic elimination campaigns and regular sapling distribution — building sustainable habits among youth.
